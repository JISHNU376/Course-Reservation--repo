ChatGPT and Generative AI

ChatGPT and Generative AI represent a groundbreaking advancement in artificial intelligence, enabling machines to create human-like text, images, and even code. ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is a conversational AI model that can understand prompts and generate context-aware, natural responses—making it useful for writing, tutoring, coding, customer service, and more. Generative AI, more broadly, refers to AI systems that can generate content, such as articles, art, music, or synthetic data, based on learned patterns. These technologies are reshaping industries by improving productivity, automating tasks, and enabling creative innovation, while also raising important questions around ethics, accuracy, and responsible use.
